ABOUT THE fastr5K PROGRAM (9 WEEKS)

Can you run your target pace for one minuate?

The fastr5K Program (9 Weeks) is a 5K training plan designed to increase your 5K speed over the span of 9 weeks. This plan is designed for everybody! Wether you are a 20 year 5K racer or just starting out, we guarantee this training plan will build you into a winner! You want to the way it works? We thought so! The success of the fastr5K Program (9 Weeks) lies in it's simplicity. Most times, training plans are too complicated, and require a great deal of extra work that you really don't need in order to break your 5K PR. The fastr5K Program (9 Weeks) alternates running with walking. For example: John Doe has a 5K PR of 23:50 which is a pace of 7:40min/mile. When John Doe jumps onto the fastr5K Program (9 Weeks), he determines the pace that he wants to run his 5K after the training plan. The only requirement for this training plan is that you are able to maintian your target pace for one minuate! So, John Doe decides that he wants to run his 5K at an average pace of 6:26min/mile, which will give him a finishing time of 19:57. Over the next 9 weeks, the fastr5K Program (9 Weeks) will alternate running and walking to build John Doe the endurance to successfully run his 5K in 19:57!

Plan Preview

"Run" means running at your target pace.

Week 1 (3 days a week)

  1. Warm Up
  2. Run for 1 minuate
  3. Walk for 1 1/2 minuate
  4. Repeat for 20 minuates
  5. Cool Down

Week 9 (3 days a week)

  1. Warm Up
  2. Run for 30 minuates
  3. Cool Down
